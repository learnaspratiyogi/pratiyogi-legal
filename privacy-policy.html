Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026  |  Effective immediately upon app use

1 Introduction

Welcome to Pratiyogi, a competitive exam preparation app designed to help students practice calculations, solve Previous Year Questions (PYQ), stay updated with current affairs, and track their performance.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. By using Pratiyogi, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

Full Name Email Address Quiz Scores Questions Attempted Practice Sessions App Activity Data Device Type
✅ What we do NOT collect: We do not collect your photos, camera, microphone, location, contacts, call logs, SMS, or any other sensitive device data. Pratiyogi operates without accessing your personal files or media.

3 How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

• To create and manage your user account securely.

• To display your performance dashboard, track scores, and show progress over time.

• To personalize your practice experience based on attempted questions.

• To maintain app security and prevent fraudulent activity.

• To improve app features and content quality based on aggregate usage patterns.

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted backend-as-a-service platform. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) which ensures that each user can only access their own data — no user can see another user's data.

All data transmission between the app and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

5 Third-Party Services

Pratiyogi uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

• Supabase — Database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)

• Google Play Services — App distribution on Android

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

6 Children's Privacy

Pratiyogi is intended for students aged 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the personal data we hold about you.

• Correct any inaccurate information in your profile.

• Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

• Export your data upon request by contacting us.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified within the app. Continued use of Pratiyogi after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We typically respond within 48 hours.
